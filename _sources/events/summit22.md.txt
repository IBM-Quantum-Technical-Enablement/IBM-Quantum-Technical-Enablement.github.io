# Quantum Practitioners Forum 2022

(summit22)=

## Overview

A workshop experience focused on demonstrating the latest in IBM Quantum software and services via hands-on demonstrations and reusable building blocks.  Attention will be paid on integration with current workflows and/or how improved workflows can offer performance advantages.

## Details

**Date**: November 10th, 2022

**Location**: 335 Madison, NYC

**Invitees**: 75 advanced users from across the IBM Quantum Network
